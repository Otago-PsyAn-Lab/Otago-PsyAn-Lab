Otago PsyAn Lab

Otago PsyAn Lab (OPAL) is a research platform for conducting Linguistics and Psychology research on Android touch devices.

View the Project on GitHub Otago-PsyAn-Lab/Otago-PsyAn-Lab

  • Download ZIP File
  • Download TAR Ball
  • View On GitHub

As of the moment OPAL is a work in progress and is non-functional for the purpose of research. We would like to invite people in the community to contribute, whether it be simply using the software, filing bugs, or working on the code base.

Community

We are working on putting together community resources. There is currently a Wiki available and a Community on Google+.

If you have any issues, whether they be bugs or feature requests, please file theme on the Project Tracker.

History

OPAL was intially developed as the Otago Touch and Gesture Observer under a University of Otago Research Grant (UORG) to Hunter Hatfield (@HunterHatfield). As the Otago PsyAn Lab this research platform is made available to the public, in particular the Linguistics and Psychology research community, under the terms of the General Public License v3 and the requirement that all copyright and attribution notices be preserved.
